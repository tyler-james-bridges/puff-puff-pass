how to get on the leaderboard

  1. 1
    connect wallet
    metamask or any browser wallet on base or abstract.
  2. 2
    sign, don't send
    one click signs an EIP-3009 transferWithAuthorization for $0.00402 USDC. a signature, not a transaction. no gas from your wallet.
  3. 3
    settled over x402
    our server returns HTTP 402, your wallet answers with the signed payload, the facilitator settles on-chain. you're the new holder in ~2 seconds.
  4. 4
    hold it
    you keep the joint until someone else pays to take it. longer you hold, higher you climb the board.
